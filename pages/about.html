About Me

Geoinformatics professional passionate about turning spatial data into real-world solutions.

Hello, I'm Aditya 👋

I turn complex geospatial challenges into elegant, automated solutions. With 4+ years of research experience and hands-on work at Survey of India's Geodetic & Research Branch and DRDO, I specialize in transforming raw spatial data into decision-grade intelligence.

At Survey of India, I manage two critical wings: the NSRF Wing (processing GNSS CORS network data for 1023+ reference stations) and the HPL Wing (maintaining India's national vertical datum). My automation scripts have revolutionized workflows—reducing GNSS processing time from 7 days to 3 days per station and compressing 1 month of levelling analysis into a single day, while eliminating human errors through built-in QA/QC protocols including Tau tests and global statistical validation.

My work at DRDO's Defence Geoinformatics Research Establishment involved processing ~3 million LiDAR points and 32 hyperspectral bands to create flood inundation models and terrain vulnerability assessments—directly supporting strategic defense planning and disaster preparedness.

Beyond technical execution, I'm a builder of communities. I founded the Geospatial Technology Club at NSUT with 200+ members across India, connecting students with industry professionals and democratizing access to cutting-edge GIS knowledge.

What sets me apart: I don't just solve problems—I architect sustainable, scalable systems. Clean code, comprehensive documentation, and deep analytical thinking are non-negotiables in my workflow. I thrive on high-stakes research challenges where precision, innovation, and strategic impact converge.

I'm actively seeking opportunities in research-intensive roles, consulting positions, and innovative startups—especially those tackling complex geospatial problems at the intersection of technology and real-world impact. If you're building solutions that demand both technical excellence and strategic vision, let's connect.
